### How the AI Engine Works (Simplified)

When you perform any action in WizMark 360 — create a social post, run an SEO audit, score a lead — here is what happens behind the scenes:

1. **Task Analysis:** The WAI-SDK orchestration engine analyzes your request — what type of task it is, which vertical it belongs to, what language is needed, and how complex it is.
2. **Agent Selection:** From the 285 available agents, the engine selects the best-fit specialist for the job (e.g., a Social Content Creator agent for Instagram posts, or a Technical SEO Auditor for site analysis).
3. **Model Routing:** The Smart Model Router picks the optimal LLM from 24 providers and 886 models based on cost, speed, quality, and capability requirements.
4. **Execution:** The agent completes the task using the selected model, applying your brand context, guidelines, and preferences.
5. **Quality Check:** Built-in guardrails validate the output against your brand voice, compliance rules, and quality standards.
6. **Delivery:** The result is presented to you for review, approval, or automatic publishing (depending on your ROMA autonomy level).

#### Choosing Your Automation Level (ROMA Framework)

WizMark 360 uses the **ROMA framework** to control how much autonomy you give the AI:

| Level | Name | What Happens | Best For |
|-------|------|-------------|----------|
| **L0** | Reactive | AI only acts when you explicitly ask it to | New users getting comfortable |
| **L1** | Proactive | AI suggests actions; you approve before execution | Teams wanting guidance |
| **L2** | Autonomous | AI executes pre-approved strategies on its own | Experienced users |
| **L3** | Collaborative | Multiple AI agents coordinate across verticals automatically | Enterprise teams |
| **L4** | Self-Evolving | AI learns from results and adapts strategies independently | Advanced power users |

> **Best Practice:** Start at **L1 (Proactive)** and gradually increase as you build confidence. You can set different ROMA levels for different verticals.

### 4.1 Social Media Marketing (45 Agents)

**What it does:** Creates, schedules, publishes, and optimizes social media content across all major platforms — Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn, X (Twitter), Pinterest, YouTube, and Threads.

**Why you need it:** Social media is the most time-consuming marketing channel. WizMark 360's 45 agents handle everything from ideation to analytics, freeing your team to focus on strategy.

#### Step-by-Step Workflow

1. **Content Ideation**
   - Navigate to **Social Media > Content Calendar**
   - Click **"Generate Ideas"** — the AI analyzes trending topics, competitor activity, and your brand voice
   - Review suggested topics, select the ones you want, and add them to your calendar

2. **Content Creation**
   - Click on any calendar slot and select **"Create Content"**
   - Choose the platform (Instagram, LinkedIn, etc.) and format (post, carousel, reel, story)
   - The AI generates copy, suggests hashtags, and can create images
   - Edit as needed, or click **"Generate Variants"** for A/B options

3. **Review & Approve**
   - All content enters your approval queue (configurable under Settings)
   - Team leads can approve, request edits, or reject with one click
   - At ROMA L2+, pre-approved content types skip the queue

4. **Schedule & Publish**
   - The AI recommends optimal posting times based on your audience analytics
   - Click **"Schedule"** or **"Publish Now"**
   - Multi-platform publishing sends to all selected channels simultaneously

5. **Monitor & Engage**
   - Real-time engagement tracking shows likes, comments, shares, and reach
   - AI suggests responses to comments and DMs (Groq-powered for sub-second speed)
   - Sentiment monitoring alerts you to negative trends or potential crises

6. **Analyze & Optimize**
   - View performance dashboards with engagement rates, follower growth, and content scores
   - AI generates weekly reports with actionable recommendations
   - Top-performing content is automatically flagged for repurposing

### 4.2 SEO & GEO Optimization (38 Agents)

**What it does:** Optimizes your website for traditional search engines (Google, Bing) AND next-generation AI search engines (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews). SEO handles Google rankings; GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) ensures your brand appears in AI-generated answers.

**Why you need it:** 60%+ of web traffic starts with a search query. With the rise of AI search (ChatGPT, Perplexity), your brand needs to be visible in both traditional and AI-generated results.

#### Step-by-Step Workflow

1. **Technical SEO Audit**
   - Navigate to **SEO/GEO > Site Audit**
   - Enter your website URL and click **"Run Full Audit"**
   - The AI checks Core Web Vitals (LCP, FID, CLS), mobile compatibility, schema markup, crawlability, sitemaps, robots.txt, canonical tags, and broken links
   - Results appear as a scorecard (0–100) with prioritized fix recommendations

2. **Keyword Research**
   - Go to **SEO/GEO > Keywords**
   - Enter a seed topic or let the AI suggest keywords based on your industry
   - View keyword difficulty scores (0–100), search volume, intent classification, and SERP features
   - AI identifies keyword gaps where competitors rank but you don't

3. **Content Optimization**
   - Select any page and click **"Optimize"**
   - AI scores your content against the top 10 SERP results
   - Get suggestions for semantic keywords, readability improvements, meta titles/descriptions, and E-E-A-T signals

4. **Backlink Analysis**
   - Navigate to **SEO/GEO > Backlinks**
   - View your link profile, identify toxic links, and discover new opportunities
   - AI generates personalized outreach emails for link-building campaigns

5. **GEO Optimization (AI Visibility)**
   - Go to **SEO/GEO > AI Visibility**
   - Track whether your brand appears in responses from ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews, and Bing Copilot
   - AI recommends content changes to increase your citation-worthiness

6. **Local SEO (India-Specific)**
   - Manage Google Business Profile, JustDial, Sulekha, and IndiaMART listings
   - AI generates review responses and handles NAP consistency across directories

#### Available Features and Tools

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| Site Audit Engine | Core Web Vitals, mobile-first indexing, schema validation |
| Keyword Intelligence | AI-powered research with difficulty scoring and intent classification |
| Content Scoring | Compare your pages against top 10 SERP results |
| GEO Tracking | Monitor brand visibility in AI-generated search results |
| Link Building | Automated outreach email generation with quality scoring |
| Local SEO | Google Business Profile, Indian directory submissions |
| Hreflang Support | Multilingual SEO for 22 Indian languages |

#### Key Metrics / KPIs Tracked

- Organic Traffic & Sessions
- Keyword Rankings (SERP positions)
- Domain Authority / Page Authority
- Core Web Vitals Scores
- Backlink Count & Quality

#### Best Practices

- Run the AI Visibility tracker weekly — your GEO score will become as important as your traditional SEO ranking

- Use Local SEO for every physical location; city + language keyword variants boost regional traffic

### Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Do I need technical or coding skills to use WizMark 360?**
A: No. The platform is designed for marketing teams and business users. Everything is point-and-click, with AI handling the complexity behind the scenes.

**Q: How do I choose which AI model to use?**
A: You do not have to. The Smart Model Router automatically selects the best model for each task based on cost, speed, quality, and capability requirements. If you want to override, go to **LLM Settings**.

**Q: Can I use WizMark 360 for multiple brands?**
A: Yes. You can set up unlimited brand profiles, each with its own identity, voice, team permissions, and analytics. This is ideal for agencies managing multiple clients.

**Q: What languages can I create content in?**
A: 22 Indian languages plus English. 8 languages have full voice support (STT + TTS). All 22 support text generation and translation.

**Q: How does the ROMA autonomy system work?**
A: Start at L0 (Reactive) or L1 (Proactive) — the AI suggests actions but waits for your approval. As you build confidence, increase to L2 (Autonomous) for approved tasks, L3 (Collaborative) for cross-vertical coordination, or L4 (Self-Evolving) for full autonomous operation. You can set different levels per vertical.

**Q: Is my data secure?**
A: Yes. WizMark 360 uses enterprise-grade security with role-based access control, audit logging, data encryption at rest and in transit, and compliance guardrails for DPDP Act 2023 (India), GDPR, and other regional regulations.

**Q: How does billing work?**
A: WizMark 360 uses Razorpay with full GST/IGST compliance. You can pay via UPI, credit/debit cards, net banking, wallets, or EMI. Monthly, quarterly, and annual billing cycles are available with automatic invoice generation.

**Q: Can I connect my existing CRM?**
A: Yes. Bi-directional integration with Salesforce and HubSpot is built in. Contacts, leads, accounts, deals, and activities sync in real-time. Custom field mapping ensures your CRM stays clean.

**Q: What if an AI agent makes a mistake?**
A: All content goes through your configured approval workflow before publishing. At ROMA L1, nothing publishes without your approval. Even at higher ROMA levels, guardrails prevent publishing content that violates brand guidelines. You can always roll back any action.

**Q: How many team members can I add?**
A: This depends on your subscription plan. Check **Settings > Billing** for your current plan limits. All plans support the 4 RBAC roles (Super Admin, Admin, Editor, Viewer).

**Q: What is the WizMark Intelligence Suite?**

See Section 5 for details.

**Q: Can I use WizMark 360 for WhatsApp Commerce?**

### Troubleshooting Common Issues

#### Connection Failed (Platform Integrations)
- **Check:** Are your API credentials or OAuth tokens still valid?
- **Fix:** Go to **Settings > Integrations**, click the failing platform, and re-authenticate
- **Still broken?** Ensure your platform account (Meta, Google, etc.) is active and has the required permissions

#### Agent Not Responding
- **Check:** Is the LLM provider online? Go to **LLM Settings** to see provider status
- **Fix:** The Smart Model Router automatically falls back to an alternative provider
- **Still broken?** Click "Reset Agent" in the Agent Registry, or contact support

#### Campaign Not Publishing
- **Check:** Is the content in "Approved" status? Go to **Content Library** and check the status column
- **Fix:** Approve the content and try publishing again
- **Still broken?** Verify your platform connection is active and check platform-specific requirements (e.g., WhatsApp template approval, Meta ad review)

#### Slow Performance
- **Check:** Are you running many concurrent operations?
- **Fix:** The platform queues tasks automatically. For time-sensitive tasks, mark them as "High Priority"
- **Still broken?** Clear browser cache or try a different browser

#### Content Not Generating in the Right Language
- **Check:** Is the correct language selected in your content creation settings?
- **Fix:** Set your default language in **Settings > Language Preferences** and ensure the correct language is selected in the content creation dropdown
- **Still broken?** Try generating content natively in the target language instead of translating

#### WhatsApp Templates Not Sending
- **Check:** Has the template been approved by WhatsApp?
- **Fix:** Go to **WhatsApp > Templates** and check the approval status. Rejected templates need to be revised and resubmitted
- **Still broken?** Ensure your WhatsApp Business API account is active and in good standing
